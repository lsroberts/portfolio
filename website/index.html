About Me

I'm Lucy Roberts, a student-journalist at Northwestern University. I graduated from Wake Forest University in 2024, where I worked for the student-run newspaper, the Old Gold & Black. I wrote across multiple sections of the newspaper, including News, Opinion, Sports and Arts & Culture. I also served as the Social Media Manager and created and published graphics for content across the organization's social media platforms.

I specialize in sports storytelling, exploring the intersection of sports and society.
